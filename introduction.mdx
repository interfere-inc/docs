Interfere is a real-time issue intelligence platform that monitors your application continuously and surfaces problems the moment they appear — not after a user files a ticket, not after support queues spike, and not after you've spent an afternoon correlating logs across five different tools. From the first signal to a confident fix, Interfere owns the entire resolution workflow so your team can focus on building instead of firefighting.

## Who Interfere is for

Interfere is designed for every person on a product team, not just the engineers writing the code.

- **Engineers** get root-cause analysis tied directly to their codebase. Instead of piecing together errors, traces, sessions, and deployment diffs manually, you see exactly which code change introduced a problem and receive a suggested fix you can act on immediately.
- **Product managers** get a real-time view of which users are affected, how a problem is growing, and how it connects to recent releases. Smart prioritization means the most impactful issue is always at the top of the inbox, so you can steer the fix without chasing engineering for status updates.
- **Designers and GTM teams** get session replays, natural-language summaries, and impacted-user charts they can read without an engineering background. Customer-facing teams can answer support questions with confidence the moment a problem is detected, often before the first ticket arrives.

## How Interfere works

Interfere follows a three-step flow that takes you from raw signal to resolved issue.

### 01 — Detect issues before your customers do

Interfere continuously monitors your app and notices when behavior or outcomes change, before errors escalate or users report issues. It tracks full-stack signals — errors, page events, session data, release markers, and more — and raises a new issue the moment something looks wrong. You learn about the problem in minutes, not hours.

### 02 — Understand what's going wrong

Interfere goes beyond logs, metrics, and traces. It finds the root cause and explains what's broken, why, and who it impacts. Every issue in your inbox comes with a timeline of findings and facts: which deployment introduced the regression, which file changed, how the error rate moved, and how many users are affected. Zero-touch triage means the right person is notified automatically so nothing waits in a queue.

### 03 — Fix problems with confidence

Interfere turns insight into action by suggesting fixes, linking them to your codebase, and tracking progress until the issue is resolved. You stay in control at every step — Interfere surfaces the recommendation, but you decide when and how to ship the fix.
