title: "Activity"
icon: "bell"
description: "A running feed of what's happening across your workspace."
---

Activity is the pulse of your workspace: a chronological feed of what Interfere and your team have been doing. New problems as they're detected, regressions and recoveries as they happen, and team events like a surface coming online or someone joining.

Use it to stay in the loop without living in the [Inbox](/product/inbox). Activity tells you what changed; the Inbox is where you act on it.
